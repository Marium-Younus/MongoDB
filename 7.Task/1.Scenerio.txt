There are three users of the system at the moment: 
Admin, Visitors, and Vet (who treated animals). The system stores login credentials for users. 
It maintains information about individual animals residing in the zoo; each animal belongs to a 
type, such as Mammal, Birds, Reptiles and Amphibians. Additionally, the diseases of animals should be maintained.

Furthermore, the data of treatments administered to animals by the vet will be stored. This includes details of each treatment, such as the treatment description, associated animal, and vet.
The system should have a collections that stores ticket prices according to age groups. Visitors can purchase multiple tickets at once, and all those tickets should be stored against a single order ID with the total amount, date, and time, etc.
